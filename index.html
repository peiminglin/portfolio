Blobby

Blobby Blob is a blobtastic 3D puzzle platformer where you play as a sentient blob that wants to explore the world. Trapped in a laboratory, you collect the hidden key cards by completing various challenges and change your shape to solve different puzzles.

This is the last University project, made by 4 Games Developments students and 2 Music & Sounds Design students. The project won The Best Video Game Award in UTS FEIT student prototype exhibition spring 2019.

UnityC#

Team : 4 design/programer + 2 sfx

Role : Programmer/Game Designer

Year : 2019

Award : The Best Video Game

Wandering Jack

Wandering Jack is a Cute 2D platform, action-adventure game based on a space background. Player control a cat, jumping among the planets and collect pieces to escape.

The project was created by a 4 members team. I was the team leader/manager and main programmer. The game was based on a physic system that I created to keep the player attracted by the planets.

A funny thing for me in the development was that the way I did for parallel space effect was from a dream I made during the project.

UnityC#2dPuzzle

Team : 2 design/programers + 1 UI + 1 Art

Role : Leader Programmer/Designer

Year : 2019

Little Planet

Little Planet was a computer graphic project that made with Three.js. User can use the keyboard to move around the planet or use the mouse to change the terrain. Through the control panel, user can also change the appearance of the planet, like colors, sand/snow/forest levels, temperature, terrain and etc.

My main contribution in this project was the camera control and the cinematic mode. To make it easier to control, the left/right keys will act differently depends on the distance.

Pursuit

The game was originally called Plato's Labyrinth and changed to Pursuit later. It was created on a 32-hours University Game Jam in 2018.

The idea was inspired by a flash game I played when I was a kid. I thought it was perfect for the theme which was "Changing Perspective". The levels were quite hard to design in a short time, so I even make a level design tool after the Game Jam finished, just in case I want to complete this game later.

This game got The Most Challenging Game Award.

Team : Myself

Year : 2018

Award : The Most Challenging Game
